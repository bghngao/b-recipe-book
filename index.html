Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water and mix in.
  5. Stir in flour and salt, then chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until edges are golden.

Notes

You can substitute walnuts or pecans for extra crunch. For a chewier cookie, slightly underbake and let cool on the tray.
